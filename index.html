Tim Berners-Lee is a software engineer who invented the internet (or the world wide web) in 1989 while at CERN, the European particle physics labortory. In 2001 he became a fellow in royal society an in 2004 he was knighted

In 1994 Berners-lee found the world wide consortium(W3C) at the labortory of computer science(ICS) at the massachusetts institute of technology in Boston.It was made up of companies that wanted to work together to make Web standards and also to improve the web.It was quickly agreed that the technology should be free of royalties and patents.Berners-Lee has served as the director of this W3C since it was founded.

In 1995 microsoft introdused its first version of internet explorer and this commercal browser created enormous growth in websites and web users.
The web was first used for e-commerce in 1997.
